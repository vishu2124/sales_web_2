Sales management system

Are you getting the most out of your sales contacts or do you feel they could be used better? A sales management system will help you track leads, manage your sales contacts effectively, and monitor the progress of your deals with ease.

What is a sales management system and why do I need it?

A sales management system is a tool that helps salespeople get organized, manage their contacts better, track their sales deals more efficiently and save them time. In short - a tool that helps salespeople sell more. A sales CRM should also give sales managers a simple yet clear overview of how their team is performing - a tool that helps their team sell better. So whether you’re a small-business owner, a salesperson or a sales manager, a good sales management software will help you drive your sales and reduce headaches.

What makes a sales management system good?

There are plenty of things you need to look out for when shopping for a sales CRM. Above all, you need to make sure that the CRM you’re going to choose suits your needs. The aspects that need to be included in a good sales CRM, according to our more than 40 000 users:

  • Easy to use
  • Simple to learn, intuitive
  • Customizable
  • Notifies you when to take action
  • Mobile – accessible everywhere.
  • Good sales reporting
  • Cross-platform integrations
  • Good looking, visual

The problem with "once-a-week CRMs"

To get the most out of a sales management system, people need to want to use it – it needs to be helpful in day-to-day business. Far too often CRMs end up being these tedious and tiresome points of data entry on the final day of the week. The problem here? People forget – they forget deals, contacts and deadlines, which makes the “once-a-week CRM” basically useless.

Eye candy, aka why good looks and ease of use make a difference

A good sales management software is visually appealing and gives the user a comprehensive view of the situation. It’s simple to use, as if customization were a religion and drag-and-drop its holy script. Think about it, every businesses are different, but most people generally like simplicity.
